## PISN — Pan-Islamic Socialist Nationalism
Islamic Socialist Nationalism
القومية الاشتراكية الإسلامية

"The Ummah is the Nation; the Earth is its Commons; Justice is its Sovereign."

A synthesis of al-Afghani's civilizational solidarity, Iqbal's metaphysics 
of collective selfhood, and the Islamic socialist tradition — forged in the 
crucible of colonial modernity.
